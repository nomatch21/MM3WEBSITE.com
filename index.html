HOLY CROSS OF DAVAO COLLEGE

Holy Cross of Davao College (HCDC) is a private, Catholic educational institution in Davao City. An institution that operates in molding students and teachers to become competent and professionals. A college that provides undergraduate degree programs with different departments, namely Business and Management Education, Maritime Education, Criminal Justice Education, Engineering and Technology, Teacher Education and Humanities, Social Sciences and Communication. A affordable catholic excellent education to all, commits itself to deliver in-depth training on online institution and services for teachers and employees. A school with 70 years of operations that renews its mission as a RENEWED, REVITALIZED AND RESTRUCTURED archdiocesan educational institution of Davao City.
