Anne Margarette Gamil
MAY 22, 2021

I am Anne Margarette Gamil, a 22-year-old and currently a third-year student at Holy Cross of Davao College, taking up a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management. It is a great privilege to study here in Davao City since I am from Davao de Oro. I am a probinsyana who is determined to pursue my dreams here in the City.

Since I was a kid, I have always dreamed of studying here in Davao. With the help and support of my dearest family, here I am today, making my dreams come true. I discovered many things and experienced different struggles in life. Sometimes I do experience failure, but when I fail, I double my effort and give my whole best to be better. In every achievement, I always owe it to God.

As years go by, I meet different people and learn from them. I discovered many stories about their life, struggles, problems, families, stand in life, and how they dealt with it. Sometimes, it made me realize that even though we do not have everything, we need to be grateful for what we have and be thankful. Reaching out to others and helping them in our own little ways will make them happy. Giving is not about the quantity that counts, but our sincerity and desire to help are what counts the most.
